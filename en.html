Method

How options structure helps read the market

Options structure helps reveal the zones where price most often changes behavior. These are not random lines on a chart, but key levels where dealer hedging and the concentration of options exposure begin to have a visible effect on the move.

Some levels act as a magnet and a price stabilizer. Others act as a barrier whose break can sharply accelerate the move. That is why on some days the market tends toward range and mean reversion, while on others it shifts toward impulse and level breaks.

For a trader this matters because such zones help you understand in advance where price may slow down, where a fight is likely and where the scenario stops working. It lets you look at the market not as chaos, but as a structure with regime, levels, scenarios and clear risk points.

This is the basis of the daily BTC, ETH and SOL scenario map. First the market regime is defined, then key levels are marked, after that the base and alternative scenarios are built, risk is set and the scenario break point is specified.
